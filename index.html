The Journey to Motherhood

There's no hood like motherhood.... or something like that.
On September 9th, 2021, I was in Kuwait and went to the doctor
for something unrelated to pregnancy. After all, I was overseas
on a restricted tour, and there was no way in heck I was pregnant.
I had to take a pregnancy test before I left the U.S., and my results
were negative. That, coupled with having no symptoms, lead me
to believe I was safe. I couldn't have been more wrong....

Not only was I pregnant, I was 13 weeks pregnant...
A whole trimester complete! I went into shock at
the clinic and could not contain myself. What was I
going to do? What was my unit going to say?


Fast forward to today, the Army was able to send me back
to the U.S. safely and just in time to see a doctor.
Because there are no obstetricians in Kuwait,
I did not see one until I was halfway through my pregnancy.
Thankfully, my results came back normal and I am just
3 weeks away from meeting my little surprise baby.
As for my unit, I was blessed to have supportive leadership
who cared about my safety and getting me back to the U.S.
Although it threw a curveball in my career,
they took care of me and took measures to protect me.
